Automatically launch the ssh-agent#

By default, ssh agent is not persistent in WSL (contrary to real linux). A solution is to launch automatically the ssh agent at the beginning of the session

eval `ssh-agent -s`
ssh-add .ssh/cao-tri.do@mazars.fr
